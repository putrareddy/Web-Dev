Pregnancy & Abuse

It is a big decision to decide whether and when to have a child with a parent. Pregnancy and parenthood cause physical , emotional, financial and social changes which may become even more challenging when your partner is abusive toward you.

If you are pregnant and are in an abusive relationship, you need to make your health a priority. We will help you put together a security plan and locate resources in your area
