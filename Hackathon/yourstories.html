Domestic violence is sometimes used as a synonym for intimate partner abuse committed by a spouse or partner in an intimate relationship with the other spouse or partner, and can occur in heterosexual or same-sex relationships, or among former spouses or partners. Domestic abuse in its broadest sense often includes violence against children, parents or the elderly. It takes a variety of forms, including physical , mental , emotional, cultural , social, reproductive and sexual violence, ranging from subtle forms of coercion to marital rape and severe physical abuse such as shock, punching, female genital mutilation
