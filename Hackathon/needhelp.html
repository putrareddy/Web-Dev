Obtaining Domestic Abuse Help And Support

Domestic violence, also referred to as domestic abuse, involves physical, mental , and sexual abuse in couple or family relationships. There can be domestic violence against anyone and someone can be an abuser.

You don't have to wait to get help in an emergency situation. When you are experiencing domestic violence, it is important to tell someone, and know that you are not alone.
